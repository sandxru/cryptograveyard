BlockFi

November, 2022

BlockFi said it could not conduct business as normal and would be limiting activity in the wake of FTX's collapse. BlockFi has currently stopped honoring client withdrawals.
FTX Exchange

November, 2022

The exchanges collapsed amid liquidity concerns and allegations of misused funds, followed by a large volume of withdrawals from rattled investors. On 11th November, 2022 FTX filed for bankruptcy protection in the U.S
Celsius Network

July, 2022

On 14th July, 2022 Celsius Network had filed voluntary petitions for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. They had paused all withdrawals, swap, and transfers between accounts on June 14th.
Voyager

July, 2022

On 6th July, 2022 Voyager annouced they had filed for chapter 11 bankruptcy. On 2nd July, 2022 cryptocurrency lender Voyager Digital suspended trading, deposits, withdrawals, and loyalty rewards.

3 Arrows Capital

July, 2022

Crypto hedge fund Three Arrows Capital (3AC) which was founded in 2012 had filed for chapter 15 bankruptcy on 2nd July, 2022.

Terra Luna

May, 2022

On May 7, the price of the then-$18-billion algorithmic stablecoin terraUSD (UST), which is supposed to maintain a $1 peg, started to wobble and fell to 35 cents on May 9. Its companion token, LUNA, which was meant to stabilize UST's price, fell from $80 to a few cents by May 12. At the peak Terra Luna and UST had a market cap around 40B and 18B respectively.
